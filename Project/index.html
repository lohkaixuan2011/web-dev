What Is
Microplastic

Microplastics are tiny plastic particles, generally defined as less than 5 millimeters in length.
They are ubiquitous in nature and have infiltrated ecosystems worldwide, making their way into oceans, drinking water, food supplies, and human

How It Affects Us

Damage to Ecosystems

Microplastics can affect coral reefs, plants, and aquatic habitats. This weakens ecosystems and reduces biodiversity, meaning fewer species can survive healthily.

Effects on Human Health

Humans may consume microplastics through drinking water and seafood. Scientists are still studying the long-term effects, but concerns include possible damage to organs, hormones, and the immune system.

Harm to Marine Life

Fish, turtles, whales, and other sea animals often mistake microplastics for food. When swallowed, the plastic can block their stomachs, reduce hunger, and cause injury or death. Tiny organisms such as plankton are also affected, disrupting the food chain.

Our Project About

Rising Temperatures

Melting Ice Caps

Our project focuses on detecting microplastics in water because increasing plastic pollution causes tiny plastic particles to enter rivers and oceans. These microplastics can be consumed by marine life and eventually enter the human food chain. Ocean Currents
& Climate

Climate Change

Climate change is the gradual increase in Earth's temperature caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels and cutting down trees. This causes the planet to become warmer, leading to problems like hotter weather, melting ice, rising sea levels, and more extreme storms. Climate change affects people, animals, and the environment around the world.

Gulf Stream

The Gulf Stream is a warm ocean current that moves heat from the Gulf of Mexico across the Atlantic Ocean and helps regulate climate, especially in nearby regions. Climate change may affect this current because melting ice adds freshwater into the ocean, changing water density and circulation patterns. If the Gulf Stream weakens, it could lead to changes in weather, rainfall, temperatures, and marine ecosystems in different parts of the world.

Sustaninable Fishing

Sustainable fishing means catching fish in a way that protects marine life and allows fish populations to grow naturally. It helps prevent overfishing and keeps oceans healthy for future generations. By using responsible fishing methods, people can continue to enjoy seafood while protecting the environment.

Marine Conservation
Efforts

Marine conservation efforts are actions taken to protect oceans and marine life from pollution, overfishing, and habitat destruction. These efforts include reducing plastic waste, creating protected marine areas, and encouraging sustainable practices. Marine conservation helps keep ocean ecosystems healthy for future generations.

Ocean Exploration

Submarines, POVs, Satellite Image

Ocean exploration is the process of studying the ocean to understand marine life, underwater environments, and changes in the ocean. Scientists explore the ocean to discover new species, study ecosystems, and learn how oceans affect Earth's climate and environment.

Scientists use different technologies to explore the ocean. Submarines allow researchers to travel underwater and observe marine life directly. ROVs (Remotely Operated Vehicles) are underwater robots used to explore deep areas safely and collect data. Satellite images help scientists monitor ocean temperature, currents, pollution, and changes in sea levels from space.

How Can You Help

Reduce single-use Plastics

Reducing single-use plastic helps keep oceans clean and protects marine life from harmful plastic pollution. It also reduces the amount of microplastics in the water, making the ocean healthier for animals and humans. By using reusable alternatives, we can help protect the environment for future generations.

Support Eco-friendly Brands

Supporting eco-friendly brands helps reduce pollution and protect the environment. These brands often use sustainable materials and environmentally friendly production methods, which create less waste and use fewer natural resources. Choosing eco-friendly products encourages more businesses to care for the planet and helps create a cleaner, healthier future.
